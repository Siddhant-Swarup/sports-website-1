A Journey Through the marvellous career of Mahendra Singh Dhoni
  • December 2004:Dhoni made his international debut in ODIs against Bangladesh in Chittagong.
  • October 2005:Sent up in the batting order to score fast runs. In his second innings, he scored 183 runs in 145 balls. India won the five-match series 3–0 and Dhoni was the man of the series.
  • December 2005:Dhoni made his Test debut against Sri Lanka in Chennai.
  • September 2007:Dhoni took the captaincy of ODI cricket from Rahul Dravid.
  • September 2007:Dhoni equaled Adam Gilchrist's international record for the most victims in ODI cricket. He became the captain of India in the first T20 World Cup in South Africa. India defeated Pakistan in the final. Dhoni got the final over of the final with a less experienced bowler like Joginder Sharma and it proved to be a masterstroke.
  • August 2008:Dhoni led India to victory in the first bilateral series in Sri Lanka.
  • August 2008:Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award to Dhoni.
  • November 2008:Dhoni became the Test captain of India. He took the captaincy from Anil Kumble in the fourth Test against Australia in Nagpur.
  • December 2008:Dhoni became the best ODI cricketer of the ICC year.
  • March 2009:Under Dhoni's captaincy, India won the first bilateral ODI series in New Zealand.
  • April 2009:Dhoni received the Padma Shri, India's fourth highest civilian honor. December 2009: Dhoni became the first player to win the ICC Best ODI Cricketer of the Year award twice in a row.
  • May 2010:Chennai Super Kings won the IPL under Dhoni's captaincy.
  • April 2011:Dhoni played an unbeaten 91-ball 79 in the World Cup final against Sri Lanka. India won the World Cup after 28 years. Dhoni Man of the Match, winning by six.
  • May 2011:CSK won the IPL under Dhoni's captaincy.
  • November 2011:The Indian Territorial Army made Dhoni an honorary lieutenant colonel.
  • March 2013:Dhoni became the most successful Test captain of India by breaking the record of Saurabh Ganguly by registering 21st victory in 49 Tests.
  • June 2013:India won the ICC Champions Trophy under Dhoni's captaincy.
  • February 2013:Dhoni scored his first double century in Test cricket.
  • March 2013:Under Dhoni's captaincy, India beat Australia 4–0 in the home Test series.
  • April 2018:Padma Bhushan to Dhoni.
  • May 2018:Chennai won the IPL for the third time under Dhoni's captaincy.
